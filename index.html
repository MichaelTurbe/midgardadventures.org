About Midgard Adventures

Midgard Adventures is a fan-organized play group for the Kobold Press Midgard Campaign setting. We communicate and plan via our discord server, play our games on the Roll20 virtual tabletop, and use discord for voice during our sessions.

The purpose of this gathering is facilitate one-shot D&D 5E and Pathfinder game sessions set in Midgard. Anyone can join by jumping in, creating a character, and showing up. Those characters can be used in other sessions, and can progress and level up. This is very much like Adventures League and Pathfinder Society, but with adventures set in Midgard.

This group is open to brand new D&D players, experienced role-players, new fans of Midgard, folks who have never played D&D or Pathfinder online with a virtual tabletop, and anyone at all interested in sharing an amazing, immersive, dark, and vibrant campaign setting with new friends from around the world.
